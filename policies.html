Policies

Shipping Policy

We provide timely delivery of your delicious and nutritious tiffin meals right to your workplace. Our delivery schedule is as follows: Monday to Friday, excluding holidays Deliveries will be made during lunch hours Please ensure that someone is available at the delivery location to receive the tiffin during the specified hours. In case of any changes in the delivery schedule, we will notify you in advance.

Return and Refund Policy

We strive to provide high-quality tiffin services. If you encounter any issues with your meal or delivery, please contact our customer support within 24 hours. We offer the following options for resolution: Replacement of the meal in case of quality issues. Refund for the affected meal if a replacement is not satisfactory. Refund requests must be made within 48 hours of receiving the tiffin. Our team will promptly address your concerns and work towards a satisfactory resolution.
