About Us

We are a team made up of human beings who love to eat good food. The story started when I first came to Pune for a job. At that time, everything was good, but the only problem I faced was about eating my lunch. In our area, there is no one who provided good food that I liked; if I liked their taste, it was very costly. One day, I had enough of it all and started 'Aapla Daba' so that I could eat good food and also provide good food to my friends.
